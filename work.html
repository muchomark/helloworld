StockXSupply

Co-founder Apr. 2019 - Present

StockX reached out to my co-founder and I in early 2019 to discuss how we could potentially work together to drive more traffic to their marketplace. After several weeks of strategizing and development, we built StockXSupply, an online feed on X (formerly Twitter) and Discord that alerts users of price drops and deals based on our defined thresholds within the StockX marketplace. Since working together, we have generated over $10M in sales for StockX, and our monitors are now hosted in more than 600 Discord communities.
